About Binangkal House

Welcome to Binangkal House, your ultimate destination for the best Binangkal treats in town. We are a locally-owned business dedicated to crafting this traditional Filipino delicacy with a modern twist. Our passion for quality and innovation drives us to offer a variety of Binangkal flavors and experiences that keep our customers coming back for more.

Our Story

Founded in 1969, Binangkal House started as a small kitchen operation, sharing our love for Binangkal with friends and family. Today, we have grown into a beloved local brand, known for our dedication to quality and flavor.

Our Mission

We aim to preserve the rich tradition of Binangkal while infusing it with contemporary flavors and ideas. Our mission is to bring joy to every bite, creating memories that our customers will cherish.

Our Team

Our team is a group of passionate bakers and food enthusiasts who share a common love for Binangkal. Each member brings their unique skills and creativity to the table, ensuring that every piece of Binangkal we make is a masterpiece.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the finest ingredients to ensure that our Binangkal is the best it can be.
  • Innovation: We continuously explore new flavors and techniques to keep our offerings fresh and exciting.
  • Community: We believe in giving back to the community that has supported us from the beginning.
  • Tradition: We honor the rich cultural heritage of Binangkal, preserving its legacy for future generations.
